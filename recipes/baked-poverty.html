Baked Poverty

Cheap and relatively easy so you can conserve your time and energy and put that focus towards the global overthrow of capitalism. Stay strong comrades!

  • 2 Packages Gradein, Plantein, or Similar Vegan Product
  • Half Package Fries
  • Salt, Pepper, Favorite Sauce
  1. Preheat oven
  2. Place "meat" and fries on parchment paper-lined baking sheet
  3. Cook according to instructions
  4. Serve hot with your choice of condiments
