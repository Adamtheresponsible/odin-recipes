Poverty Noodles

Cheap and relatively easy so you can conserve your time and energy and put that focus towards the global overthrow of capitalism. Stay strong comrades!

  • 5 Packs of Mr. Noodles
  • 2 Packages Smoked Tofu
  • 1 Package Frozen Corn
  • Peanut Butter
  • Sriracha
  1. Bring large pot of water to boil
  2. Dice tofu
  3. Add noodles, cook until softened
  4. Strain out most of the water
  5. Add seasoning packages, peanut butter, sriracha, and corn
  6. Cook until desired
  7. Serve hot, enjoy
