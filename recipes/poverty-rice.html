Poverty Rice

Cheap and relatively easy so you can conserve your time and energy and put that focus towards the global overthrow of capitalism. Stay strong comrades!

  • 2 Cups Minute Rice
  • 2 Packages Tofu
  • 1 Bag Frozen Vegetables
  • Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Your Choice of Seasonings
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Nooch (Nutritional Yeast for the uncultured)
    • Onion Powder
    • Garlic Powder
    • Etc.
  1. Dice tofu
  2. Chop and sautee onions and garlic in oil
  3. Cook rice
  4. Add tofu and soy sauce to onions and garlic and cook until firm
  5. Add frozen vegetables to tofu and cook thoroughly
  6. Add cooked rice and seasonings to tofu and cook as much as desired
  7. Serve hot
